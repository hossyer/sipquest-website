üîí Privacy Policy

How we protect your data

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to SipQuest! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our multiplayer drinking game app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Names: We collect the names you choose to display in games
  • Game Sessions: Information about game sessions you create or join
  • Game Activity: Your participation in games and responses to prompts

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Connection Data: IP address and connection timestamps for multiplayer functionality
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app (features used, session duration)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide multiplayer gaming functionality
  • To synchronize game state across all players in a session
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To ensure fair play and prevent abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Other Players: Your chosen display name and game actions are visible to other players in your game session
  • Service Providers: We use cloud services to host our multiplayer infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using WebSocket connections
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Game Sessions: Deleted when the session ends or all players leave
  • Connection Logs: Retained for up to 30 days for troubleshooting
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized for long-term analysis

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection

7. Age Restrictions

SipQuest is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud hosting and multiplayer infrastructure
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Payment processing (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. International Users

If you are using SipQuest from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sipquest.app
  • Subject: Privacy Policy Inquiry

By using SipQuest, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
